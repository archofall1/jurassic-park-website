A Dinosaur Guide

Tyrannosaurus Rex

One of the largest and most famous carnivores. Known for its powerful roar and massive size, it's the undisputed king of Jurassic Park!

Velociraptor

A very intelligent and agile predator that hunts in packs. They are smaller than a T-Rex but much smarter and faster.

Triceratops

A large, plant-eating dinosaur with a giant frill and three horns on its face. It's a gentle giant of the park.

Brachiosaurus

A massive herbivore known for its incredibly long neck, which it used to reach leaves high up in trees. It's one of the most peaceful dinosaurs.

Pteranodon

A flying reptile with a long, crested head and a large beak. While not a true dinosaur, it was a memorable resident of the aviary.

Ankylosaurus

A dinosaur known for its incredible defense. It had a bony, club-like tail and armor plates covering its body, protecting it from predators.

Spinosaurus

An enormous carnivore that was even larger than the T-Rex. It had a huge sail on its back and was known to be a powerful swimmer.

Dilophosaurus

A venomous carnivore that could spit at its prey. It was known for the frill around its neck and the two bony crests on its head.

Gallimimus

A fast and agile dinosaur that looked similar to an ostrich. They were known for running in large flocks and being difficult to catch.

Stegosaurus

A famous herbivore with large, bony plates on its back and a spiked tail for defense. It was a gentle but powerful dinosaur.

Compsognathus

A tiny, turkey-sized carnivore that hunted in huge packs. Despite their small size, they were a dangerous threat in groups.

Mosasaurus

A massive marine reptile that lived in the park's lagoon. It was the top predator of the sea, known for its powerful jaws and huge size.

Indominus Rex

A large, genetically engineered hybrid dinosaur created by InGen. It was known for its high intelligence and camouflaging abilities.

Allosaurus

A ferocious carnivore with powerful legs and sharp claws. It was a major predator in its time, known for its small horns above its eyes.

Carnotaurus

A unique carnivore with two small horns above its eyes and incredibly small arms. It was known for its speed and powerful bite.
