Jurassic Park and Jurassic World Movies & Series

Explore the entire movie saga and the exciting animated series that expand the Jurassic universe.

The Movies

Jurassic Park (1993)

The very first movie! A group of scientists and a lawyer are invited to a new theme park with real, living dinosaurs. But things go wrong when the power fails and the dinosaurs escape, forcing them to find a way to survive and escape the island.

The Lost World: Jurassic Park (1997)

Four years after the events of the first movie, a second island where the dinosaurs were bred is discovered. A new team is sent to study the dinosaurs, but they must race against another group that wants to capture the dinosaurs and bring them to the mainland.

Jurassic Park III (2001)

Dr. Alan Grant is tricked into returning to Isla Sorna, the second dinosaur island. He and his team must survive new dangers and a ferocious Spinosaurus as they try to rescue a lost boy and get off the island.

Jurassic World (2015)

Decades after the original park, a new theme park called Jurassic World is open to the public. Everything is going great until a new, genetically-modified dinosaur escapes and causes chaos across the island.

Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom (2018)

The volcano on Isla Nublar is about to erupt, threatening to wipe out all the dinosaurs. Owen and Claire return to the island to save as many dinosaurs as they can, but they uncover a bigger conspiracy that could change the world forever.

Jurassic World: Dominion (2022)

Four years after the destruction of Isla Nublar, dinosaurs now live and hunt alongside humans all over the world. The balance between man and predator is tested in a world where humanity must figure out how to live with these prehistoric creatures.

The Animated Series

Jurassic World: Camp Cretaceous (2020-2022)

An animated series following a group of six teenagers chosen for a once-in-a-lifetime experience at a new adventure camp on the opposite side of Isla Nublar. They must work together to survive and escape the island after the dinosaurs break loose.

Jurassic World: Chaos Theory (2024)

This is the sequel to Camp Cretaceous. Years after escaping Isla Nublar, the former campers are young adults and face new challenges in a world where dinosaurs are now everywhere. They must uncover a conspiracy that threatens both humans and dinosaurs.
