The Movies

Jurassic Park (1993)

The first film that started it all! Scientists are invited to a new theme park on a remote island, but chaos erupts when the cloned dinosaurs escape.

The Lost World (1997)

Four years after the disaster, a new team travels to a second island where the dinosaurs are thriving on their own, but they face a new danger.

Jurassic Park III (2001)

A new adventure on the second island with bigger and smarter dinosaurs. This time, the team is searching for a lost boy.
