The Movies and Series

Jurassic Park (1993)

The iconic film that started it all, where scientists are invited to a theme park of cloned dinosaurs, leading to a catastrophic power failure.

The Lost World: Jurassic Park (1997)

A new team travels to a second island where dinosaurs have been left to thrive, but a plan to bring them to the mainland threatens everything.

Jurassic Park III (2001)

A desperate search and rescue mission on Isla Sorna, where a team discovers a new breed of deadly dinosaurs.

Jurassic World (2015)

A new, fully functioning park opens to the public, but a genetically modified dinosaur escapes and wreaks havoc across the island.

Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom (2018)

When the island's volcano erupts, a team races to rescue the dinosaurs, but they uncover a conspiracy that puts humanity at risk.

Jurassic World: Dominion (2022)

Dinosaurs now live alongside humans, and the two generations of Jurassic heroes team up to save the planet from a new, bio-engineered threat.

Jurassic World: Camp Cretaceous (2020-2022)

An animated series following a group of teenagers who get stranded on Isla Nublar after the dinosaurs escape, forcing them to survive on their own.

Jurassic World: Chaos Theory (2024)

Another animated series that continues the story of the Camp Cretaceous survivors as they navigate a new world with dinosaurs living on the mainland.

Jurassic World: Rebirth

A thrilling new chapter in the Jurassic saga, where a new generation of heroes must confront a world now dominated by dinosaurs, leading to a clash of survival and extinction.
