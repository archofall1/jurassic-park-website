Jurassic World Video Games

Learn all about the exciting games where you can build and manage your very own dinosaur park!

Jurassic World Evolution

Released in 2018, this game lets you take charge of operations on the legendary Isla Nublar and the surrounding islands of the Muertes Archipelago. As the head of the park, you are responsible for building attractions, containment, research, and breeding new dinosaurs. Your choices will impact the success of the park and the well-being of your dinosaurs and guests!

Jurassic World Evolution 2

This sequel, released in 2021, builds upon the first game with a new campaign, more dinosaurs, and new ways to manage your park. It features a new Chaos Theory mode where you can play through "what-if" scenarios from the films. You'll face new challenges and stunning new environments as you work to protect and contain your dinosaurs.
